In this very special episode, hosts Brendan Craven and I sit down with Jayson E. Street, a world-famous hacker and seasoned veteran in the red teaming field. Jayson is known for his successful high-profile security awareness engagements, having infiltrated banks, hotels, government facilities, biochemical companies, and more institutions around the world. He’s also spoken at DEF CON, DerbyCon, and several other events on a variety of InfoSec subjects. Over the course of an hour, Jayson shares his valuable insights and experiences with everyone, recounts some of his most memorable stories, and imparts lessons on becoming a better security auditor. This episode is packed with expert advice and real-world hacking tales from one of the industry’s best.
