Think NXT Proximus

Proximus Think NXT is a one-day event focused on innovation in technology. The most recent event, which I attended, Think NXT 2023, took place on November 30th, 2023. The next event, Think NXT 2024, is scheduled for Thursday, November 28th, 2024 at Nekkerhal, Mechelen.

The event features keynote speakers, interactive demos, breakout sessions, and networking opportunities. It’s designed to be a place to learn about new technologies, exchange ideas with other attendees, and get inspired by industry leaders.

Think NXT 2023 at Tour & Taxis in Brussels was a whirlwind of innovation and inspiration. As someone deeply invested in the future of technology, I had the chance to connect with some of the biggest names in the industry, and let me tell you, it did not disappoint!

AI & Cybersecurity Front and Center

One of the hottest topics was the intersection of AI and cybersecurity. At the Google booth, discussions revolved around the new LLM model, Sec-palm 2. It seems AI is not just a threat, but a powerful tool in the fight against cybercrime. They also shed light on Operation Aurora, a fascinating glimpse into Google’s efforts to combat state-sponsored attacks.

Security Solutions Galore

Fortinet showcased their impressive security product suite, along with their highly regarded incident response team. They emphasized the importance of being prepared for breaches, not just preventing them. Cisco, on the other hand, focused on their user-friendly Meraki system, a great option for businesses seeking a streamlined network management solution.

Palo Alto Networks: A Three-Pronged Approach

Palo Alto Networks offered a comprehensive perspective on cybersecurity with their “3 Pillars of Security” framework. Their Unit 42 incident response team was another highlight, demonstrating their expertise in handling security breaches. They even introduced LEAP, a new academic program designed to cultivate future cybersecurity talent – a fantastic initiative!

Beyond the Big Names

I wasn’t limited to the biggest players. Infoblox impressed me with their focus on DNS security and incident response. KnowBe4 offered a unique solution with their phishing platform, empowering companies to train employees to identify and avoid phishing attacks.

A Day of Learning and Connection

Think NXT 2023 was an invaluable experience. I left the event brimming with insights into the latest advancements in AI and security, and with a renewed sense of optimism about the future. The opportunity to connect with industry leaders and fellow tech enthusiasts was truly inspiring. If you’re in the IT field, I highly recommend attending Think NXT next year – you won’t regret it!
