About Me

Welcome to my beautiful website where I will share my professional journey in the field of Cybersecurity!

I am deeply passionate about cybersecurity, with a focus on creating and maintaining secure environments where users and organizations can protect their personal and sensitive data from malicious threats. I have a strong appreciation for Open-Source principles, believing they are crucial for advancing technology, enhancing transparency, increasing security, and boosting efficiency.

One of my key strengths is my ability to stay updated with the latest advancements in the field of cybersecurity. By regularly engaging with industry literature, professional forums, and relevant events, I ensure that I am well-informed about new developments and emerging threats. This enables me to effectively anticipate and counteract potential security issues, providing robust protection for the data and systems I oversee.

My educational and professional career started in 2017 when I started a 4 year programming course at the Tambov State Technical University (TSTU) where I acquired and honed my skills in software development. After graduating, I moved my gaze towards Cybersecurity by enrolling in a Cybersecurity bachelor’s course at Howest University, where for the past 3 years I have been deeply involved with security in different facets of IT. During my studies at Howest, I was fortunate enough to receive an internship offer from Inuits, a company that prides themselves with providing open-source business solutions, which made me passionate about Open-source software.
